    Runbook

    Support workflow.

    01

    Define the impact

    Confirm who is affected, what changed, and whether the issue is public access, internal access, degraded service, or data risk.

    02

    Locate the layer

    Map the symptom to networking, compute, identity, storage, monitoring, or application health before changing anything.

    03

    Check the basics

    Review route tables, security groups, IAM roles, service health, logs, metrics, and recent configuration changes.

    04

    Document the fix

    Record the symptom, cause, evidence, change made, and prevention step in clear language for future support handoff.
