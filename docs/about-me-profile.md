## Apple-Style Profile Copy

KhanhNhat Pham focuses on IT systems, cloud infrastructure, and the operational logic behind reliable support environments. His work direction connects technical troubleshooting, cloud architecture study, and infrastructure-minded problem solving.

Before entering digital infrastructure, he built a strong foundation in physical operations and logistics. Across Charlie's Produce and HungryPanda, he worked in high-pressure inventory control, warehouse workflow, route coordination, and supply-chain support, developing a practical sense for system stability and resource allocation.

He is now advancing that operations background through structured IT and cloud training at Per Scholas. His current technical track includes operating systems, computer hardware fundamentals, Linux, Python, networking, AWS architecture concepts, and preparation for AWS Certified Solutions Architect - Associate (SAA-C03).

With multilingual communication strengths and cross-functional experience, he aims to bridge business operations and technical teams. His belief is simple: reliable systems are built by people who understand both the infrastructure and the users depending on it.
